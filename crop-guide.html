How to Apply Organic Fertilizers

Application method matters as much as which fertilizer you choose. Follow these general guidelines.

🌿
Before Sowing

Mix compost or FYM thoroughly into the topsoil 2–3 weeks before sowing. This gives time for nutrients to integrate into the soil and for microbial activity to activate.

🌿
During Growth

Apply vermicompost as a side-dressing alongside growing crops. Keep it 3–4 cm away from the plant stem to avoid root burn and encourage outward root growth.

🌿
Neem Cake Usage

Mix neem cake into the soil at the base of each plant before transplanting. Reapply every 45 days for continuous pest protection and nitrogen supply throughout the growing season.
